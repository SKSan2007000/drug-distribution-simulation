Abstract

This project presents a real-world inspired simulation of drug distribution in the human body using transform-based mathematical modeling and computational visualization. The prototype uses a grid model where every cell stores concentration values. The drug starts from the stomach region and spreads through body regions based on diffusion, directional flow, and elimination parameters. The system provides live visualization and region-wise concentration output for brain, blood, stomach, and liver.
