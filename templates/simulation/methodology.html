Methodology

Mathematical and Computational Approach

01

Drug Initialization

The drug is introduced into the stomach region as an initial concentration value based on selected dosage.

02

Diffusion

Concentration spreads from each grid cell to neighboring cells, representing drug distribution through tissues.

03

Directional Flow

Flow strength gives a preferred movement direction, inspired by vector field behavior and circulation.

04

Elimination

Drug level reduces gradually to represent metabolism and removal, especially linked with liver function.

Model Interpretation

C(t+1) = Distribution by Diffusion + Directional Flow − Elimination

This is a simplified numerical implementation of time-based concentration change. Laplace transform concepts are used in the theoretical explanation of solving such time-dependent differential models.
